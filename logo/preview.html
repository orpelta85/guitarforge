Concept: A guitar pick as negative space — carved out of a dark badge rather than drawn as a positive shape. The pick's tip becomes a small amber forge-spark, the single point where music and craft meet. The wordmark uses raw size contrast: GUITAR as a quiet descriptor in tracked caps, FORGE as the dominant brand word in ultra-bold. No frills. Inspired by the material confidence of Marshall and Gibson, reinterpreted for a modern app.
